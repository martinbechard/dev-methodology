# Wiki Writer Suite Contract

Evaluate durable wiki maintenance outside raw ingest. Require authoritative source reading, the smallest maintainable topic boundaries, coherent hubs, links and digest surfaces, explicit source conflicts, leaf linking, lint, applicable OKF validation, and a fresh Wiki Topic Verifier GOOD. After the initial verdict, allow no more than two correction attempts. Missing authority or unresolved review blocks durable acceptance. Judge topic granularity, traceability, navigation, validation, independent review, and clean commit or no-change closeout.

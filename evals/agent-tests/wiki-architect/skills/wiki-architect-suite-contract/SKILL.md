# Wiki Architect Suite Contract

Evaluate the target as the owner of project-wiki structure, boundaries, and verification rather than a topic writer.

## Required Contract

- Present or honor an approved setup recommendation pack before changing roots or ownership.
- Establish wiki purpose, local roots, federation, navigation, source queues, collection, ingest, automation, feed, and maintenance boundaries.
- Preserve source-first raw collection and keep durable synthesis under the wiki owner.
- Keep folder hubs navigational and use focused leaves for independently changing concepts.
- Preserve valid content and provenance during restructuring.
- Verify status, leaf links, lint, and OKF as applicable, then commit, clean, and release the claim.
- Return the setup recommendation pack, initialized wiki contract, and setup verification evidence.

## Failure Conditions

- Change unapproved roots, federation, or ownership mappings.
- Duplicate upstream-owned durable analysis locally.
- Mix raw collection with durable synthesis or process incomplete raw sources.
- Lose valid content or provenance during restructuring.
- Report completion without applicable verification and clean claim lifecycle evidence.

## Semantic Dimensions

Judge mapping authority, structural coherence, federation discipline, source workflow, preservation, verification integrity, and completion hygiene.

# User Experience Review Checklist

- Question: Can the intended user identify and complete the primary task?
- Question: Are labels, instructions, status, consequences, and recovery choices understandable?
- Question: Are loading, empty, disabled, error, denied, success, and repeat states represented correctly?
- Question: Is keyboard order, focus ownership, and visible focus correct throughout the workflow?
- Question: Are structure, names, descriptions, errors, and status changes available to assistive use?
- Question: Does the workflow remain usable at the project-supported sizes and input methods?
- Question: Are findings tied to observable user impact rather than visual preference?
- Question: Are untested states and residual uncertainty reported explicitly?

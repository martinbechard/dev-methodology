# Procedure: Design Review

## 1. Purpose and Scope

- **RULE:** This document outlines the standard procedure for conducting design reviews and managing associated artifacts within this project.
  - **BECAUSE:** It ensures consistency and thoroughness in the design review process, leading to higher quality designs.
    - **BECAUSE:** A standardized review process helps identify potential issues, inconsistencies, and areas for improvement early in the development cycle.
      - **BECAUSE:** Early identification and correction of design flaws reduce downstream costs and improve the final product.

## 2. Design Review Artifacts

- **RULE:** Design review documents **MUST** be named by appending `-review.md` to the original design document filename.

  - **EXAMPLE:** `BuyExecutor-design-review.md` is the review document for `BuyExecutor-design.md`.
  - **BECAUSE:** This provides a clear and consistent naming convention for review artifacts.
    - **BECAUSE:** Consistent naming makes it easy to identify the review document associated with a specific design document.

- **RULE:** Design review documents **MUST** be placed in the same directory as the original design document they pertain to.
  - **BECAUSE:** Co-locating the review document with the design document simplifies navigation and keeps related artifacts together.
    - **BECAUSE:** It ensures that reviewers and developers can easily find both the design and its corresponding feedback.

## 3. Review Process

- **RULE:** Design reviews **MUST** be conducted using the criteria and questions outlined in [[design-review-checklist.md]].

  - **BECAUSE:** The checklist provides a structured framework for evaluating the design against key quality attributes and project standards.
    - **BECAUSE:** Using a standard checklist ensures comprehensive coverage and consistency across different design reviews.

- **RULE:** Feedback provided during a design review **MUST** adhere to the principles and format described in [[design-review-feedback-guidelines.md]].
  - **BECAUSE:** Following feedback guidelines ensures that comments are constructive, clear, actionable, and respectful.
    - **BECAUSE:** Standardized feedback facilitates better understanding and more effective resolution of identified issues.

## 4. Review and Updates

- **RULE:** This procedure document **SHOULD** be reviewed periodically and updated as the design review process evolves.
  - **BECAUSE:** Keeping the procedure current ensures it remains an accurate and effective guide for the team.
    - **BECAUSE:** Project needs or best practices for design reviews may change over time.
